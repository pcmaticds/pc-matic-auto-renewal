Automatic renewal is a convenient feature that helps keep your security software subscription active without requiring manual renewal each year. However, some users may prefer to manage renewals themselves. This **How to Stop PC Matic Auto Renewal** guide explains the general process of managing your subscription settings, reviewing billing preferences, and understanding what happens after automatic renewal is disabled.

Turning off automatic renewal does not immediately cancel your current subscription. In most cases, your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, you can decide whether to renew your subscription manually.

This guide also covers common questions, possible issues, and helpful tips for managing your subscription with confidence.

Why Users Turn Off Auto Renewal
===============================

There are several reasons why customers choose to disable automatic renewal.

Common reasons include:

* Preference for manual renewals.
* Updating payment methods before renewing.
* Reviewing subscription options before the next billing cycle.
* Changing personal budgeting preferences.
* Reducing automatic recurring charges.

Choosing manual renewal gives users more control over when and how their subscription is renewed.

Things to Know Before Turning Off Auto Renewal
==============================================

Before changing your renewal settings, keep the following points in mind:

* Your current subscription generally remains active until its expiration date.
* Turning off automatic renewal does not remove the software from your computer.
* Security features available under your current subscription usually remain accessible until the subscription expires.
* You can review your renewal preferences again later if your needs change.

Understanding these details helps you make an informed decision.

How to Stop PC Matic Auto Renewal
=================================

The exact steps may vary depending on your account interface, but the general process is similar.

Step One
--------

Open your preferred web browser and sign in to your PC Matic account using your registered credentials.

Step Two
--------

Go to the **Account**, **Subscription**, or **Billing** section of your account dashboard.

Step Three
----------

Locate your current subscription information and review the renewal settings.

Step Four
---------

Find the option related to **Automatic Renewal** or **Auto Renewal**.

Step Five
---------

Select the option to turn off or disable automatic renewal.

Step Six
--------

Review any confirmation message and save your updated preferences.

After completing these steps, verify that your account reflects the updated renewal setting.

How to Verify Your Renewal Settings
===================================

After making changes, it is a good idea to confirm that the update was successful.

You can generally do this by:

* Signing back into your account.
* Opening the subscription or billing section.
* Reviewing your renewal status.
* Confirming that automatic renewal is shown as disabled if that is your preference.

Checking your account helps ensure your changes have been applied correctly.

Common Issues When Disabling Auto Renewal
=========================================

Unable to Find Renewal Settings
-------------------------------

If you cannot locate the renewal option:

* Make sure you are signed in to the correct account.
* Review all subscription or billing sections.
* Refresh the page and try again.

Changes Not Saving
------------------

If your settings are not updating:

* Check your internet connection.
* Sign out and sign back in.
* Try again using an updated web browser.

Subscription Status Still Shows Active
--------------------------------------

This is normal in many cases. Turning off automatic renewal usually does not cancel the remaining subscription period. Your subscription may stay active until the current term ends.

Billing Information Needs Updating
----------------------------------

If you are changing your payment details, update your billing information before making future subscription decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions
==========================

Does turning off auto renewal cancel my subscription?
------------------------------------------------------

No. In most cases, disabling automatic renewal only stops future automatic billing. Your current subscription generally remains active until the end of the existing subscription period.

Can I renew my subscription later?
----------------------------------

Yes. If automatic renewal is disabled, you can usually choose to renew your subscription manually before or after it expires.

Can I turn auto renewal back on?
--------------------------------

Yes. If you later decide to use automatic renewal again, you can typically update your renewal preferences through your account settings.

Why can't I find the auto renewal option?
-----------------------------------------

The location of subscription settings may vary depending on your account interface. Make sure you are signed in to the correct account and review the available billing or subscription sections.

Do I need to uninstall PC Matic after disabling auto renewal?
-------------------------------------------------------------

No. Disabling automatic renewal does not remove the software from your device. The application generally remains installed unless you choose to uninstall it.
